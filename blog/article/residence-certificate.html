How to apply Residence Certificate in Bihar

By Tech 2torials • Mar 2026 • 5 min read

If you`r home in bihar and yout want to create your domicile/residence certificate in bihar.dont worry Tech 2torials hepl you to how you can apply residence certificate in Bihar.

What is residence/domicile certificate?

Residence/domicile Certificate is a proof of you residence or proof address. it used many places

Required Documents

  1. Aadhaar Card
  2. Passport size photo
  3. Mobile Number

Step by Step Process

Follow these steps to apply residence certificate online:

Visit the official RTPS Bihar Bihar website, select apply for residence certificate and fill all required details carefully. Upload documents and submit.

How to Check Application Status

You can track your residence certificate application using reference number on RTPS BIhar portal.

Tip: Always use correct details as per Aadhaar to avoid rejection.
