How to Apply Voter ID Online

By Tech Tutorial â€¢ Feb 2026 â€¢ 5 min read

Applying for a voter ID card online is very easy. The Election Commission of India provides an official portal where citizens can apply from home.

Who Can Apply for Voter ID?

  • Indian citizen
  • Age 18 years or above
  • Valid address proof

Required Documents

  1. Aadhaar Card
  2. Passport size photo
  3. Address proof

Step by Step Process

Follow these steps to apply voter ID online:

Visit the official NVSP website, select Form 6 and fill all required details carefully. Upload documents and submit.

How to Check Application Status

You can track your voter ID application using reference number on NVSP portal.

Tip: Always use correct details as per Aadhaar to avoid rejection.
