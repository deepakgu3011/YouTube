Google Form Without Email Login

By CodeCraft Pro ‚Ä¢ Feb 2026 ‚Ä¢ 4 min read

Google Forms is widely used for surveys, registrations and feedback. In many cases, you may want users to submit a form without signing in. This is useful when collecting public or anonymous responses.

What is Google Form Without Email Login?

A Google Form without email login allows anyone to submit responses without using a Google account. Users do not need Gmail or sign-in access.

Why Disable Email Login?

  • Public surveys & polls
  • Anonymous feedback
  • Easy access for all users
  • No Google account required

Step by Step: Create Google Form Without Email Login

  1. Open Google Forms
  2. Create a new form or open existing one
  3. Click on Settings (‚öôÔ∏è)
  4. Go to Responses tab
  5. Disable Collect email addresses
  6. Turn OFF Limit to 1 response
  7. Click Save
Important: ‚ÄúLimit to 1 response‚Äù must be OFF to allow no-login access.

How to Share Form Publicly

After disabling email login:

  • Click on Send button
  • Select Link icon
  • Copy and share the link anywhere

Difference: With Login vs Without Login

  • With Login: Verified users, limited responses
  • Without Login: Public access, more responses

Advantages

  • Easy & fast submission
  • Higher response rate
  • No Google account needed

Disadvantages

  • No email verification
  • Possible duplicate or fake responses

Best Use Cases

Google Forms without login are best for:

  • Public feedback forms
  • Event polls
  • Opinion surveys
  • Website contact forms

Conclusion

Creating a Google Form without email login is simple and very useful for public data collection. Just remember to disable email collection and response limits before sharing the form.
